Chapter 8

CSS3 Transforms and Transitions

  • Transforms
    • Most of the transformations are straight forward and simple code.
    Some key things to remember
    altering a width , height or font-size alters the space allocated for the element and can affect the layout.
    scale() does not alter the space allocated for the element.
    transformations are applied in the order provided
    1. Translation
    2. Scaling
    3. Rotation
    4. Skew
    5. Transitions
